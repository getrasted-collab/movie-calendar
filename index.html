How it works (2026)
  • Calendar is 2026 only. Anything dated outside 2026 is hidden from the timeline/calendar.
  • All: shows movies + TV episodes together (movies use theatrical dates).
  • Digital: shows movies only using their digital dates.
  • TV Episodes: only episodes in 2026, and only the newest season that has 2026 dates.
  • Header collapses when you scroll down so the add bar disappears.
  • Mini calendar: starts on months. Tap a month → see dotted days → tap a dotted day to pop up posters.
  • On phone: Mini calendar lives behind Mini Calendar button.
